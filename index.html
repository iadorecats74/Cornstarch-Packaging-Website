What is Cornstarch Packaging?

Cornstarch packaging is a biodegradable alternative to plastic. It's made from natural, renewable resources — the same cornstarch you might find in your kitchen! When disposed of properly, it breaks down into harmless organic materials that nourish the earth.

Why Choose Cornstarch?

  • 🌿 100% Biodegradable and Compostable
  • 💚 Made from Renewable Resources
  • 🐢 Safe for Wildlife and Oceans
  • ♻️ Low Carbon Footprint

The Benefits

✅ Breaks down in less than 12 weeks in compost!
🌱 Reduces plastic waste and saves marine life!
🚛 Lightweight, strong, and easy to use!

How You Can Help

Switch to products packaged in cornstarch-based materials. Spread the word about biodegradable alternatives and encourage your school, home, and community to go green!
